OBJECTIVE
BIG DATA is being generated by  
everything around us at all times. Every  
digital process and social media exchange  
produces it. Systems, sensors and mobile  
devices transmit it. Big data is arriving  
from multiple sources at an alarming  
velocity, volume and variety. To extract  
meaningful value from big data, you need  
optimal processing power, analytics,  
capabilities and skills.

The objective of this Seminar is to  
provide a forum for researchers,  
educators, students to exchange ideas, to  
communicate and discuss reseach findings  
and new advances in BIG DATA.
